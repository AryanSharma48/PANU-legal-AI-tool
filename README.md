# ⚖ PANU – AI Powered Legal Petition Drafting Platform

PANU is an AI-powered platform that helps users generate **court-ready legal petitions and documents** from simple natural language input. The goal is to make legal drafting easier and improve access to justice for people who may not have legal expertise.

---

## 🚀 Problem

Before filing a case in Indian courts, individuals must prepare legally formatted documents such as petitions, affidavits, and annexures.  

Challenges faced by most people:

- Legal formats are complex and difficult to draft
- Most people depend on expensive lawyers
- Court filing procedures are confusing
- Existing e-filing systems only allow document uploads but do not help create them

---

## 💡 Solution

PANU simplifies legal drafting by converting plain user input into structured legal documents.

Users can:

1. Select the case type
2. Fill in a simple questionnaire
3. Generate a court-ready legal draft
4. Review and edit the document
5. Download the final PDF for filing

---

## ✨ Features

- AI-powered legal petition drafting
- Court-format ready document generation

## 🔄 Workflow

1. User logs into the platform  
2. Starts a new draft  
3. Enters case details and parties involved  
4. Selects jurisdiction and case type  
5. AI generates a structured legal draft  
6. User reviews and downloads the final document  

---

## 🎯 Vision

PANU aims to make legal systems more accessible by enabling anyone to generate legally structured documents without requiring advanced legal knowledge.
